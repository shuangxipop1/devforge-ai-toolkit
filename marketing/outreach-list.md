# Newsletter Outreach List

## Target Criteria
- Developer-focused newsletters
- 5K-50K subscribers (micro-influencers)
- Active on Twitter or have clear audience
- Open to guest posts or affiliate deals

## Outreach Template

**Subject:** Quick question about [topic from their recent post]

**Body:**
Hi [Name],

I loved your recent post on [specific topic].

I'm building a practical guide for developers who want to build AI agents. The guide includes 6 learning modules, 5 production templates, and a complete 5-day quick start.

Would you be open to [choose one]:

1. **Guest post** - I'd write a 1,000-word guide for your audience on [related topic]
2. **Product swap** - I'd love to offer your subscribers [X] free access + [Y] discount
3. **Quick mention** - If you think it would help your audience, happy to provide a discount code

Happy to share the landing page so you can preview first.

Thanks,
[Your name]
@devforge (Twitter)
