### Key Insights for $100K-1M Revenue

1. **Price Point**: $19-49 converts best (not $99-299)
2. **Platform**: Gumroad > Direct payment (trust matters)
3. **Audience**: Can start small (1K followers) if targeted
4. **Product**: Must solve SPECIFIC pain point (not "be more productive")
5. **Traffic**: SEO + Twitter > paid ads for beginners

### What Actually Works:
- Solve ONE specific problem extremely well
- Price at $19-49 for digital templates/guides
- Use Gumroad for payments
- Build in public on Twitter/YouTube
- Micro-influencer outreach (10K-100K followers)

### What Doesn't Work:
- Generic "AI productivity suite" ❌
- Direct crypto payments (no buyer protection) ❌
- $99-299 pricing for unknown seller ❌
- "21 templates" as main value prop ❌
