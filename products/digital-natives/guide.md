- Cyberbullying (it's happened or will happen)
- What they see online that you can't control
- Their digital footprint (it's permanent)

### Teaching Digital Citizenship
More important than "don't share personal info":
- How to be kind online
- How to spot misinformation
- How to have a voice, not just an opinion
- Protecting their identity before it matters
