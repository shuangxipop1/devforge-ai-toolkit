## What You'll Learn

### The Presence Framework (Not Time Management)
**The problem:** You think you don't have enough time.
**The truth:** You have time. You're just wasting it on things that don't matter.

Learn to identify:
- The 20% of parenting that creates 80% of the connection
- What your kids actually need from you (it's not more stuff)
- How to end work so you can start being home

### The After-Work Transition (5 minutes)
The switch from "work mode" to "dad mode" doesn't happen automatically.

The ritual that clears your head in the driveway so you walk in ready to connect.

### The Two-Hour Window
How to structure the evening so the kids get what they need:
- **30 minutes:** Undistracted play (no phone)
- **30 minutes:** Dinner together (no screens)
- **30 minutes:** Bedtime routine (bath, books, connection)
- **30 minutes:** Your own time (recharge)

The key isn't doing more. It's being present for the right things.

### The Weekend Reset
Weekends are precious. Learn to protect them:
- One sacred family activity per weekend
- How to handle "I want to work on Saturday"
- Creating memories that don't cost money

### When Work Sneaks In
Client calls. Deadlines. The email you have to send.

How to handle work intrusions without destroying the evening:
- The boundaries that protect your family
- How to communicate limits to colleagues
- Negotiating flexibility you didn't know you had

### The Long Game
You're not just raising kids. You're shaping humans.

The big-picture questions:
- What values do you want to teach?
- How do you talk to them about money, failure, effort?
- What do you want them to remember about childhood?
